Telegram bot: TaggsBot

Description

Telegram bot is used for tagging media files for quick sending it in a chat. Bot tags your photos and stickers. Just send photo or media to the bot and he will ask you about tags for these medias. After saving you can get your pictures by tags in any chat. Just input

@taggsBot tag othertag ...

and bot will show you your pictures.

Quick start

Start bot

Start bot (find taggsBot between users or click by @taggsBot if you use web client)

Tag it

Send photo or sticker to @taggsBot and input your tags.
Don't forget do it in chat with @taggsBot

In chat with somebody just input
@taggsBot tag othertag ...
and @taggsBot will show you your tags
If you want to see and select from all your tags - just input
@taggsBot %

Edit your tags

In chat with @taggsBot select needed media and send it back to bot. Here you can input new tags, completely delete your picture or just cancel operation.
When bot will ask you about new tags - just press button "/delete" or input:
/delete
